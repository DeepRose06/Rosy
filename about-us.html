HI THERE, I'M JESSY

Bragging on our new bakery, great location near Willows Shopping Centre. Our baker is an artisan-baker and was trained in Paris. Some of our current customers are James Cook University, the Cowboys and Telstra!


I started Sweetest Menu on a whim in 2020, but now I couldn’t imagine my life without it ❤️ This is where you will find all my favourite recipes. They have been tested, developed and eaten in my very own kitchen.

My bakes are heavily inspired by my travels. In 2011, my husband and I took our first trip to the States and our lives were changed. We tried all kinds of things we had never had before, from banana pudding at Magnolia Bakery to red velvet cake, smores milkshakes and New York cheesecake.

There were so many flavours and combinations I had never seen or tasted. I soon became obsessed with American-style baking.

The fact that people from all over the world are making my recipes and sharing them with their family and friends is what gets me up in the morning. Over the years, I’ve learnt just how important dessert is, and how impactful a good recipe can be on someone’s life.
